What is LoreLock?

Only the best tool for creating and sharing creative works, whether it be for a TTRPG, story, or just for fun!

LoreLock was created by FictionFarm in 2022, whose main goal as a company is to facilitate the creation of amazing fictional works. This place is both a tool and a community, so don't be scared to ask for feedback on anything you create!
